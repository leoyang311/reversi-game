Strategy Transcript for Reversi Game
====================================

Player: Black (Computer)
Strategy: Simplest Strategy (e.g., CaptureMaxPiecesStrategy)

Initial Board Configuration:
- The board is set up with the initial pieces in the center.

Chosen Move:
- The simplest strategy evaluates the available moves for the Black player.
- It selects the move that captures the maximum number of White pieces.
- If there are multiple moves capturing the same number of pieces, it chooses the uppermost-leftmost move.
- For the starting configuration, this might typically be a move adjacent to a White piece.

Result:
- The chosen move is executed, flipping the adjacent White pieces to Black.
- The board is updated accordingly, and it's now White's turn.
